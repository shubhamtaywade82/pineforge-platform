# PineForge Vision

PineForge is an engineering platform for TradingView Pine Script.

It enables traders and developers to build production-ready strategies using AI-assisted workflows.

---

## Goals

- Accelerate Pine development.
- Improve strategy quality.
- Reduce repetitive work.
- Preserve human oversight.
- Enable production-grade workflows.

---

## Target Users

- Pine developers
- Systematic traders
- Trading teams
- Enterprises

---

## v1.0 Focus

Pine engineering workflows.

Not quantitative research.

---

## Out of Scope

These belong to future releases:

- Walk-forward analysis
- Monte Carlo simulation
- Portfolio analytics
- Broker execution
- Live trade journaling
